about me

Hi! I'm Vy (she/her), a computer science graduate from UC Berkeley currently based in Anaheim, CA.

My passion lies in developing friendly products for all. Over the course of my career, I've had the pleasure of working closely with many colleagues from various underrepresented groups. As a member of T-Cal, I'm driven to create software that delivers an inclusive user experience. This has led me to pursue full-stack development and software engineering -- roles that allow me to blend thoughtful design with robust engineering. My favorite work lies in creating apps that are not only aesthetically pleasing, but also meticulously built for accessibility, performance, and usability.

In my days off, I enjoy baking new recipes, crocheting animals, and exploring Hyrule for Korok seeds .
