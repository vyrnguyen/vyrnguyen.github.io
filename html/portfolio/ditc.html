Dinner in the Closet

In this role-playing game, you'll be trying to survive a Desi family gathering as Nidhi, a closeted queer teenager. Based on the team's personal experiences, we hope this story sheds some light on this perspective through comedic dialogue options and minigames with your occasionally-unpleasant relatives.
