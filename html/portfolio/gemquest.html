GemQuest

At Cal, students who are gender minorities (GeMs) and people of color often struggle with finding their own communities. GemQuest is a social networking app aimed to assist these groups. Students can post and join in-person hangouts called "quests," to connect with similar peers that match their preferences. While doing so, users earn rewards in the form of "gems," which act as reputable and beautiful flairs!
