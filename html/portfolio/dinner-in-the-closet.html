Dinner in the Closet

In this role-playing game, you'll be trying to survive a Desi family gathering as Nidhi, a closeted queer kid. As you pick through various dialogue options and attempt to beat your relatives in minigames, we hope this story sheds some light on what it is like to be closeted in a domestic setting.
